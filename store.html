ABOUT OUR STORE

Our stores located in many different cities with wide options of guitars.

Since 1996 we have been Rocking!

Since 1996,Guitar Music Center has been the people's premiere spot for learning guitar.Founded by Music Director & owner Taylor Lautner,through the years GMC has added electric bass guitar,banjo,dobro,lap steel,uke,piano,keyboards,violin,dulcimer,voice,drums & percussion in all of styles,levels and techniques.GMC specializes in private music lessons,group classes & performance for students of all ages.

Since 1996 Our Mission has been very clear!!!

To encourage and inspire people of any age to learn a musical instrument & share the joys of playing Music with others....

Services we offer at our store

From guitars,drums,amps and pedals to DJ gear,sound equipment and a wide range of accessories,Guitar Center has it all,and by the most well-known brands.If you like shopping for great used gear,then you'll definitely want to check out our selection of previously-owned instruments and amps.Guitar Music Center also buys and trades,so feel free to bring something in and have our staff take a look at it.All this along with free "Recording Made Easy" workshops every Saturday morning,make it quite obvious that we love all things music-related here at Guitar Music Center.
for more information about services contact 5138977554

Repairs

Like all Guitar Center locations,we also offer comprehensive repair services at our stores.Our techs are as passionate about your instruments as you are,with the experience needed to keep them performing at their best.Whether you need a quick adjustment or a complete overhaul,we've got the equipment and know-how to take care of it.The repairs department would love to hear from you:in fact,we encourage it! Call or come in for a face-to-face chat about the work that we do.You'll have no doubt that your gear is in good hands when it's time for a repair.

What to Expect

Above all else at Guitar Center South Austin,we strive to give you the experience that Guitar Center is known for nationwide:big-store selection and prices with small-shop expertise and personality.From sales and repairs to workshops and more,our staff in every department is well-trained to cater to Central Texas music-lovers.Our store is open every day of the week,so there's always a right time to visit even if you're on a busy schedule.

We offer lessons to all age students with all type of skill levels at flexible timing
