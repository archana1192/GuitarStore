Welcome to the world of music

Austin's music scene is one of the most renowned in the nation. Not only do we boast a diverse range of bands that span countless genres, but we're also the home of South By Southwest, a film, interactive, and music festival held each year in mid-March. For this reason alone, it's no surprise that Guitar Center South Austin features an impressive variety of musical instruments and pro audio gear, as well as a friendly expert staff who love helping you find your tools of the trade. In fact, our goal is to create an environment where our local musicians can feel relaxed as they shop.

We strive to give you the experience that Guitar Center is known for nationwide.

We even have an exclusive offer for the first time buyersof guitar. We teach guitar lessons with a 50% off who purchase a guitar at our store.
